## Priority Summary

**Must-run dungeons (BiS items):**
1. **The Mechanar** — Beast Lord Helm, Abacus of Violent Odds
2. **The Steamvault** (Normal + Heroic) — Beast Lord Mantle, Leggings, Wrathtide Longbow
3. **The Botanica** — Beast Lord Cuirass, Stormreaver Warblades
4. **Shattered Halls** — Beast Lord Handguards
5. **The Black Morass** — Hourglass of the Unraveller
6. **Shadow Labyrinth** — Sonic Spear
7. **Auchenai Crypts** — Claw of the Watcher

**Worth running for alternatives:**
- Old Hillsbrad (Heroic) — Nightfall Wristguards
- Slave Pens (Heroic) — Midnight Legguards (best raw leg stats)
- Underbog (Heroic) — Dunewind Sash
- Sethekk Halls — Ravenclaw Band
- Mana-Tombs (Heroic) — Wastewalker Leggings
